Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity.

What We Do

Working with youth ministry is a privileged opportunity to guide young people in faith and character. It is all about building relationships, developing spiritual growth, and creating a non-threatening situation where young people feel comfortable asking questions, discovering God's love, and developing their gifting. As we walk alongside them on their journey.

Worship & Prayer

Worship God in truth and spirit (John 4:24) and to offer our requests unto Him in prayer with thanksgiving (Philippians 4:6). Both of them lead us into His presence.

Bible Study & Teaching

"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path" (Psalm 119:105). Study and reading the Word helps to build faith and live by His truth.

Fellowship & Events

"For where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them" (Matthew 18:20). Fellowship and event make us stronger in oneness, joy, and love for Christ.

Mentoring & Counseling

"Therefore encourage one another and build a one another up" (1 Thessalonians 5:11). Through mentoring and counseling, we give wisdom, encouragement, and spiritual growth in Christ.
